Welcome to Sacho Satram Rice Mill

Sacho Satram Rice Mill is a modern rice processing factory focused on quality, purity and customer satisfaction. We use advanced machines and skilled manpower to deliver high quality rice to the market.

About Our Rice Mill

A rice mill is a manufacturing unit where paddy is processed into edible rice. At Sacho Satram Rice Mill, we follow proper cleaning, de-husking, polishing and packing procedures to ensure premium quality rice.

Rice Processing System

  • Paddy Cleaning and Drying
  • Husk Removal
  • Rice Polishing
  • Quality Checking
  • Packing and Storage

Season Information

  • Sari Season: October to January
  • Kerik Season: February to June
